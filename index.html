About Us

Vi_Arts & Design Studio is a company based in Kigali, Rwanda.
That offers Art & Designs in different forms. We bring your idea and dreams to reality, by brainstorming with coordinate efforts.

As designers, We enjoy bridging the gap between functionality and design. Our goal is to always design anything Artistic related that is scalable and eye touching under the hood while providing engaging, pixel-perfect user experiences.
In addition, We are highly responsive to client needs and also committed to helping people realize their vision.
We are at your service.
